Support FAQs

How do I install BrowserBox on my system?
BrowserBox supports macOS, Linux, and Windows. Download the installer from our GitHub releases page and follow the instructions in our Deployment Guide. If you encounter issues, contact support@dosaygo.com.
I’m having trouble activating my license—what should I do?
Ensure you’ve purchased the correct license type (commercial or non-commercial) and that your license key is valid. Refer to our Licensing FAQs for common issues, or email support@dosaygo.com with your license details for assistance.
Can I use BrowserBox for automated testing with Puppeteer?
Yes, BrowserBox is optimized for automation with Puppeteer and Playwright. Check our documentation for setup instructions. For specific configurations, reach out to support@dosaygo.com.
My organization needs custom support—how can we get priority assistance?
Enterprise customers with a commercial license receive priority support. Contact support@dosaygo.com to discuss your needs, or reach out to sales@dosaygo.com to upgrade your license for premium support options.
