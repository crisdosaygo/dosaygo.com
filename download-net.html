Archive the Web, Offline and Private

DownloadNet (also known as DiskerNet or dn) lets you save your web browsing locally, creating a searchable, offline archive. Perfect for researchers, privacy enthusiasts, and anyone who wants to revisit web pages without an internet connection.

Privacy First: All data stays on your device.
Full-Text Search: Find anything in your archive instantly.
Seamless Integration: Works with Chromium-based browsers like Chrome and Edge.
