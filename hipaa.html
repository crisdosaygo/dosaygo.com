Safeguard Patient Data with Confidence

Healthcare organizations must comply with HIPAA to protect sensitive patient data. BrowserBox Shield offers a self-hosted remote browser isolation (RBI) solution tailored to HIPAA requirements, ensuring secure browsing and robust data protection.

With BrowserBox Shield, hospitals, clinics, and healthcare providers can mitigate web-based threats while maintaining compliance with HIPAA’s stringent privacy and security rules. Our platform minimizes PII exposure and provides audit-ready controls for peace of mind.

How BrowserBox Shield Meets HIPAA

FIPS-Encrypted Data

Secures patient data with FIPS 140-3 validated AES-256 encryption for logs and configurations, aligning with HIPAA safeguards.

Data Loss Prevention

Prevents PII leaks with read-only browsing and granular DLP policies, ensuring compliance with HIPAA’s privacy protections.

Audit Trails

Generates encrypted, timestamped logs of user actions and security events, exportable to SIEM for HIPAA-compliant auditing.

HIPAA-Aligned Policies

Enforces role-based access control and signed policy documents to meet HIPAA’s administrative and technical safeguards.

Self-Hosted Control

Deploy on your infrastructure to maintain data sovereignty, with flexible integration for healthcare IT systems.

Minimized PII Exposure

Reduces PII collection and exposure during browsing, supporting HIPAA’s data minimization requirements.
