Licensing FAQs

Can we modify a previously released "Permissive License" version of BrowserBox or its precursors to meet modern standards?
Modifying older, permissively licensed versions is strongly discouraged and unsupported. Such actions risk infringing on intellectual property rights, as updates may inadvertently incorporate enhancements exclusive to our current paid licenses. Compliance with U.S. IP laws is critical, regardless of development location, based on usage and organizational residency. We advise procuring a current license for legal and operational assurance.
We’re a small organization in a resource-limited region seeking non-commercial use—why are your licenses priced as they are?
Our non-commercial license begins at $39 per user per year, designed to be accessible while sustaining development. We’re committed to supporting diverse needs—please contact sales@dosaygo.com to discuss tailored options that deliver value and maintain compliance.

We’re here to assist. Contact us at sales@dosaygo.com to adjust your seat allocation and transition to a fully compliant license. This upgrade includes access to premium support and customization, enhancing your operational capabilities.
With in-house developers, why can’t we customize BrowserBox and remove licensing restrictions ourselves?
Removing licensing mechanisms or producer identifiers violates our terms of service and applicable laws in many jurisdictions. For legitimate use, we recommend acquiring the appropriate license to ensure compliance, support, and access to updates—avoiding legal and ethical risks.
